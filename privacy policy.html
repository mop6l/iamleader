Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Leader in Me® Charity, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website, programs, and services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information (e.g., name, email address, phone number)
  • Demographic information
  • Educational information related to our programs
  • Donation information
  • Website usage data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for purposes including:

  • Providing and improving our educational programs
  • Communicating with you about our services and updates
  • Processing donations and maintaining financial records
  • Conducting research to enhance our educational initiatives
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Partner schools and educational institutions
  • Service providers who assist in our operations
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to certain data processing activities
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and collect usage information. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting children's privacy. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website.
